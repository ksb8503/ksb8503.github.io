Meet the Photographer

Blake Epstein is a Photographer and Videographer in Southern California based out of Murrieta, CA. He earned his degree in Digital Media from Mt. San Jacinto College and was mentored by multiple experienced professionals. With an eye for cinematic lighting and composition, Blake spends his time working on short films and photographing professional portraits. Now he is offering his services to those who share his appreciation of the artful nature of photography.
